MOBI Mission Impossible: Training Exercises

Team:
You have been chosen to help track down the corporate spies hiding in our midst. To do so, you must first complete agent training. Agent training consists of a series of exercises, each designed to hone your skills.
Each team must complete 3 of the 6 exercises to begin their mission. Each of the completed exercises must be performed by a different team member. You may choose any of the 6 exercises to complete. The completed exercises must be documented in video format using your agent assigned iPad. Each exercise must be completed within 60 seconds. You may retry an exercise as many times as you need. The timer should be visible in your documented video. As always, should you or any of your team be killed or injured during training, MOBI will disavow any knowledge of your actions. Good luck.
